# Collect RSVP for an event

Someone is organising an event — a birthday, a team meetup, a club fixture —
and needs to know who is coming before they book a venue, order food, or set
a squad. They want to reach invitees on whatever messaging platform those
invitees already use, get a fast yes/no/maybe, and keep a running count
without chasing people individually.

The job is done when the organiser can see, at a glance, who is attending —
and invitees can change their answer up to the deadline.
