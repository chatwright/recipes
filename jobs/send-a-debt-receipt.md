# Send a debt receipt

One person has settled a debt with another — repaid a loan, paid back a
shared expense, cleared a running tab — and both sides want a clean,
timestamped record that the amount changed hands, sent straight into the
conversation they already use rather than a separate app neither of them
opens.

The job is done when the payer has confirmation the payment was recorded
and the person owed has an unambiguous receipt they can refer back to.
