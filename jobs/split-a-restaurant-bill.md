# Split a restaurant bill

A group has just eaten together and one person paid the bill. Now someone
needs to work out who owes what — splitting evenly, or itemising who
ordered what, factoring in a shared appetiser or an uneven tip — and get
everyone to agree on the numbers without a slow back-and-forth in a group
chat.

The job is done when each participant knows their exact share and the payer
has a clear list of who still owes them.
